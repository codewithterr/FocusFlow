[[Aditya's Idea]]

> *"A focused 
> productivity app built for students to study better not harder"*

**Problem:**
Students often struggle to stay focused while studying due to constant distractions like phone notifications, lack of structured planning and no clear way to track progress. I have experienced the same problem as a student.

**Pain Points:**
- Gets distracted by phone frequently 
- Finds it hard to stay focused for long periods 
- Lacks motivation due to no visible process

**Solution**
FocusFlow is a minimal study app designed to help students stay focused, manage their time and track their progress effectively.

**Key Features**
- Focus Mode : reduces distractions during study sessions
- Study Timer : helps manage time using focused intervals
- Daily Goals : allows users to plan their task
- Progress Trackers : shows study consistency and improvement

**Feature Decisions**

- Focus Mode (App Blocking Concept):
	-Minimizes distractions by limiting access to other apps during study sessions.

- Streak Tracking: 
	-Encourages Consistency by rewarding daily study habits.

- Simple Task Checklist:
	-Keeps the interface minimal and easy to use without overwhelming the user.

- Session Controls(Pause/End):
	-Gives user flexibility without breaking their flow completely.

**More Ideas to Implement**

- AI Based Study Planner:
	-Automatically suggest study schedules based on user habits.

- Smart breaks:
	-Notifies users when to take breaks to avoid burnouts.

- Cross Platform:
	-integration through webapps along with Android and iOS forks.

- Personalized Insights
	-Store local app usage behavior and notice patterns for better workflow habits.
